Valuation Analysis - Comparable Companies Method

Comparable Companies Method

  • The Comparable Companies Method (“CCM”) of the Market Approach employs the use of ratios from publicly traded companies. These ratios were adjusted for the differences in size using a quantitative model, prior to comparing the multiples for growth and profitability for the Company. We utilized a guideline public company method as an indicator of fair market value of the business enterprise value of the company.

Search Criteria

  • Information on potential publicly traded comparable companies was ascertained using Capital IQ. Our search for publicly listed companies included companies that were related to the span of services that the Company provides. The results of our findings were discussed with Management to finalize on the list of comparable public companies that would be selected for the purposes of our analysis.
