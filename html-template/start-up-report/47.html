Appendix 2 - Key scope limitations and responsibilities

Scope and responsibilities of work under this engagement does NOT include the following:

  • Scope does not include valuation for SEBI or FEMA or tax or transfer pricing or Companies Act purposes or for any regulatory purposes in India or Outside India.
  • Scope does not include financial, tax, legal, regulatory, transfer pricing, commercial, technical diligences or accounting review or review for meeting requirements under any auditing standards or SEBI or FEMA.
  • Scope is restricted to valuation services and does not include advisory on any technical or accounting or regulatory aspects.
  • We rely on management inputs and scope does not include independent verification of the information, audit or review of the documentation. Advisor / Valuer would not be responsible for any errors, inaccuracies or inadequacies in the information provided to us. Although we will read, analyze and discuss the Management Business Plan for the purpose of undertaking our analysis, we will not comment on the achievability and reasonableness of the assumptions provided to us save for satisfying ourselves to the extent possible that they are consistent with other information provided to us in the course of the assignment.
  • We must emphasize that realization of projections will be dependent on the continuing validity of assumptions on which they are based. Our analysis, therefore, will not, and cannot be directed to providing any assurance about the achievability of the final projections. Also, since the projections relate to future, actual results are likely to be different from the projections because events and circumstances do not occur as expected, and the differences may be material.
  • Scope does not include support in negotiations internally within the management team or existing investors or with proposed investors or any third party.
  • Scope does not include independent assessment of businesses constituting a Cash Generating Unit or under any accounting regulations. We will rely on Management input for rationale and identification of Cash Generating Units.
  • Verification or Valuation of tangible assets is not covered in the scope.
  • Pursuant to this Engagement, Advisor/Valuer did not perform any Management functions for You or make any decisions. Our recommendations are based on findings from our analysis and You would be free to exercise judgement based on your experience and further reviews (if deem appropriate) for the final decision making and accepting responsibility for the results. Additionally, Management would be responsible for designating a management-level individual(s) responsible for overseeing the services provided, evaluating the adequacy of the services provided, evaluating any findings or recommendations, establishing and maintaining internal controls, and monitoring ongoing activities. The Client will comply with all applicable legal and regulatory provisions in any relevant jurisdiction.
  • No copyright violation is intended.
  • There may be minor differences in results of computations mainly on account of rounding off.
